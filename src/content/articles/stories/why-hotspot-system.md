做内容创作时，我发现「知道什么正在发生」和「知道什么值得写」是两件事。

Insight Radar 的起点很简单：把多源热点聚合起来，再用 AI 过滤出与我关注领域真正相关的内容。

这篇文章记录产品动机、第一版架构，以及为什么最后选择了 Express + React + WebSocket 的组合。

---en---

When creating content, I learned that knowing what's trending and knowing what's worth writing are different problems.

Insight Radar started as a simple idea: aggregate hotspots from multiple sources, then use AI to surface what's truly relevant to my niche.

This post covers the motivation, v1 architecture, and why I landed on Express + React + WebSocket.
