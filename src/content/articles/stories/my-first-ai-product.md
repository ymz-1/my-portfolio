第一个 AI 产品往往不是为了「改变世界」，而是为了回答一个具体的问题：我能不能用 AI 把重复写作流程缩短一半？

AI 爆款文章生成器从一次周末实验开始，逐步演化成多 Agent 流水线。本文回顾从 demo 到可用产品的几个关键节点。

---en---

My first AI product wasn't about changing the world — it answered a concrete question: can AI cut my repetitive writing workflow in half?

The AI article generator started as a weekend experiment and grew into a multi-agent pipeline. This post looks at the milestones from demo to something usable.
